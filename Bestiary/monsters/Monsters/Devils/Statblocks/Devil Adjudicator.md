>
> | **Magic, Ranged** |     **Main action** |

>
> **Effect:** The target makes a Presence test

>
> | **Magic, Ranged** |           **Triggered action** |
> | ----------------- | -----------------------------: |
> | **📏 Ranged 5**   | **🎯 The triggering creature** |
>
> **Trigger:** A creature targets the adjudicator with a strike.
>
> **Effect:** The target makes a Presence test
